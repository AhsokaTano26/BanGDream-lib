[Poppin'Party 21st Single「Drive Your Heart」](https://bang-dream.com/discographies/4102)/[10th Anniversary LIVE「星之鼓动」Blu-ray](https://bang-dream.com/discographies/4101)为纪念同步发售，将在全国门店举办Poppin'Party陈列展示比赛。
此外，针对举办陈列展示比赛的门店，还将举办Poppin'Party 21st Single「Drive Your Heart」/ 10th Anniversary LIVE「ホシノコドウ」Blu-ray同步发售纪念视听展。
